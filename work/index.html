TOP Feature

What You Get With Skillsz

Practical, Real-World Programming Questions to Help You Recruit the Top Developers

Want to hire a programmer? You have come to the right place. Finding talent is the initial step - next, you need to test their programming skills.

Although our developer screening platform can't completely replace the interview process, it can save your time by helping you prevent wasting time talking to inefficient developers.

All you do is choose the skills you want to evaluate, and we will automatically create an exam for the developer based on those skills and send it to the candidate via a secure link. Once an applicant finishes the online skill assessment test, you will instantly receive a detailed report via email. Then you can decide whether it is worth spending additional time or effort on the candidate.

Custom Branding

Do you want to ensure the skill assessment test should be consistent with your design and branding? No problem! We allow you to use your company's domain and logo without any mention of Skillsz. Your candidates will have the impression these programming assessment tests are all yours!

Printable PDF Reports

After a candidate completes a skill test, you extract the analytics for all the candidates who attended the skill test and the specific examination separately. These reports are provided in PDF format, making them easy to read, share and print.

Realtime Dashboard

We offer a simple real-time dashboard that allows you to track who has completed the test and who hasn't. Our one-click reminder system will enable you to send reminders in bulk or one at a time. You can manage the pool of your candidates very quickly while having the flexibility to keep adding new developers to the team.

Shared Permanent Link

Skillsz provides Shared Permanent Link. This type of link will allow your candidates to enter their names and email address before starting the test. You can share this link with all your candidates, publish the link on your website, or include it in a test listing.

Unmatched Prices

Our development skills testing services are designed to be cost-effective for businesses of all sizes. Our pricing structure will never create a hole in your pocket. Please let us know if you find better prices elsewhere.
